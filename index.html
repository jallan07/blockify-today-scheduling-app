Schedule your day, one block at a time!

Blockify breaks up your work day into small, one-hour blocks so you can have a clearer view of your day.

To SAVE a task to one of your blocks, enter your task information into the corresponding text areas and click the "plus" icon to the right.

To DELETE a task to one of your blocks, just click the "minus" icon to the right.

As the time of day progresses, past hour blocks will be greyed out, the current hour will be highlighted in green, and future hours will appear in blue.
